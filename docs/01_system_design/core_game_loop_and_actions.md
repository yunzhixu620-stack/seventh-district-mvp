# Core Loop And Actions

Choose a role, read a private objective, enter the rain-lit block, spend limited composure and favors on interactions, judge whom to trust, and commit a final reveal or protection before the network seals the record.

| Action | Cost | Reward | Risk / Failure |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ask | 0 composure | Public context or a soft lead | Vague reply if target lacks permission. |
| Probe | 1 composure | Private clue or contradiction | Exposure increases when the question overreaches. |
| Disguise | 1 favor | Lowers exposure and opens guarded responses | Failed mask sharply increases exposure. |
| Trade | 1 favor | Acquires a clue or cooperation | Target may publicly signal suspicion. |
| Follow | 1 composure | Reveals movement/location evidence | Target notices surveillance. |
| Reveal | No resource cost | Final claim or public evidence play | Premature reveal ends with a weak result. |
| Protect | 1 favor | Secure the envelope or a role | Protecting the wrong party costs the outcome. |

Buttons and typed input produce the same `ParsedAction`, previewed before it is committed.
