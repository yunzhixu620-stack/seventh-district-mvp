# Role Slot Model

| Slot | Surface | Private drive | Player selectable |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Investigator / 调查者 | Volunteer mediator | Establish a defensible truth without exposing the vulnerable. | Yes |
| Keeper / 保管者 | Community archive clerk | Keep the envelope sealed unless trust is established. | Yes |
| Seeker / 争夺者 | Courier looking for a missing delivery | Obtain the envelope before its meaning becomes public. | Yes |
| Protector / 保护者 | Quiet neighborhood volunteer | Redirect scrutiny away from the endangered person. | Future slot |
| Leaker / 爆料者 | Anonymous channel operator | Force enough fragments public to trigger action. | Future slot |
| System Proxy / 系统代理人 | Municipal alert terminal | Preserve procedural order, sometimes at human cost. | Future slot |

Each structured role stores public and hidden identity, goal, possible faction, private knowledge, resources, weaknesses, playable actions, success/failure rules, AI/human policies, difficulty, fantasy, and replay hook.
