# MVP Information Architecture

1. Landing: mood, premise, start, and language/audio controls.
2. Role selection: three playable motivations with difficulty and fantasy.
3. Briefing: public identity, secret objective, resources, and opening lead.
4. Situation board: fixed identity sidebar, central incident/action workspace, intelligence sidebar for public/private/clue tabs.
5. Report: result, active truth, consequential actions, missed clues, and replay with new seed.

The board exposes more than conversation: objectives, location, risk, resources, known evidence, and public events remain visible while messages arrive.
