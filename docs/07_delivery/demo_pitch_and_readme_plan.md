# Demo And Pitch Pack

## One-Minute Pitch / 一分钟介绍

《第七街区：真人测试》是一款单人也能体验社会博弈的都市推理游戏原型。林夏失联后，一封信把六种带有私心的身份拉进同一条街区频道。玩家可以按推荐动作行动，也可以自由表达意图；系统先解释玩家要做什么，再根据既定事实裁定代价和后果。它不是固定答案剧情，也不是随意聊天：每局真相由数据种子确定，角色只能基于自己知道的信息行动，结局报告会揭示你被谁误导、错过什么，以及为何下一局可能完全不同。

`Seventh District: Real Test` is a solo social-deduction prototype in which one missing contact and one sealed envelope pull six motivated roles into an urban incident. The player speaks or chooses an action; the system interprets intent, rules consequences against fixed truth, and reveals the hidden social structure only in the closing report.

## Three-Minute Demo Script

1. Show the landing screen, bilingual switch, and opt-in audio: position the product as a moody playable incident, not a chat app.
2. Select Investigator and read the private goal: establish that the player must protect safety as well as find truth.
3. Open the situation board: point out persistent role goal, exposure/resources, scene locations, action desk, and public/private intelligence.
4. Probe the Keeper with a recommended action; then type a freeform probe directed at the Protector. Explain the preview-before-commit ruling contract.
5. Open the clue tab and make a final protection/reveal choice. In the report, reveal the active truth and missed evidence.
6. Restart with a new seed: explain how role slots and structured truth provide future expansion to AI or human-controlled play.

## Portfolio Highlights

- Data-driven truth and motivated role-slot model.
- Constraint-respecting provider boundary for future AI expression.
- Rules-first free text interaction and post-session replay.
- Bilingual, responsive web interface with opt-in procedural sound.
- Automated build, rule, UI, desktop/mobile journey, and Pages deployment checks.
