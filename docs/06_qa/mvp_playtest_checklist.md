# MVP Playtest Checklist

- Three roles start with distinct goals and complete a session.
- Typed intent previews a normalized action before any resource changes.
- Buttons and typed actions produce consequential state transitions.
- Multiple seeds show different canon truths and report outcomes.
- Public activity and private contact make other roles feel active.
- Exposure, composure, and favors are legible at every turn.
- Chinese and English switching preserves the current run.
- Sound remains off until enabled, then offers ambience and feedback with a mute control.
- Keyboard controls, focus states, mobile panels, and reduced-motion preference remain usable.
- End report explains truth and missed clues and invites replay.
