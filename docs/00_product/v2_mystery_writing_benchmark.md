# Version 2.0 Mystery Writing Benchmark

## Goal

V1 action results described system state. V2 results must instead make the player feel that a person reacted, that something changed, and that one concrete detail is now worth testing.

## Reference Principles

| Reference | Principle learned | V2 use | Boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [The Case of the Golden Idol design interview](https://www.gamedeveloper.com/design/case-of-the-golden-idol) | A satisfying deduction needs sound logic and more than one observable route toward a conclusion. | Released clues are concrete and replayable; free-form dialogue changes delivery but cannot manufacture evidence. | No copied scenes, wording or puzzle structure. |
| [Return of the Obra Dinn](https://obradinn.com/) | A roster and record transform scattered observations into identity questions. | The cast is introduced before role selection; the pocket notebook records verified facts. | No copied visual language or case mechanics. |
| [Dead Meat - Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2628740/Dead_Meat/) | Interrogation has tension when the player may phrase their own challenge. | Any typed line becomes a tactic preview followed by a target-specific AI response. | This story is nonviolent and owns its own characters. |
| [Blood on the Clocktower](https://www.bloodontheclocktower.com/) | Motive, partial knowledge and post-game reconstruction sustain social deduction. | Private roles and a replay report remain structural truth, outside LLM authority. | No copied roles or rules. |

## Writing Rules Applied

1. Begin with a sensory beat or visible behavior, then state the mechanical consequence; avoid office-system phrasing.
2. Every successful investigation result identifies what became testable: a seal, timestamp, route or contradiction.
3. AI dialogue may evade, accuse, bargain or soften according to pressure; it may reveal facts only from already released evidence.
4. The consequence strip explicitly shows exposure, resource cost and new evidence so dramatic language never hides rules.
5. Chinese is written as restrained urban suspense; English is separately voiced rather than mechanically translated.

## Acceptance Check

- Opening immediately names the missing person, the envelope and all six present roles.
- A player can select a visible target portrait before speaking.
- The first investigative action produces one vivid authored consequence, one visible evidence alert and one live AI performance.
- The replay report remains deterministic and agrees with released clues regardless of generated phrasing.
